# SpendLens Economics

SpendLens is designed as a high-efficiency lead generation engine for Credex. By providing a free, high-value utility (the AI Spend Audit), we capture high-intent users at the exact moment they are looking to optimize their engineering budgets.

## 1. WHAT A CONVERTED LEAD IS WORTH TO CREDEX

A converted lead for Credex represents a significant one-time or recurring revenue event based on credit brokering and optimization. The following table breaks down the unit economics of a single customer conversion:

| Metric | Value | Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Average Startup AI Spend | $2,000 / month | Baseline for Series A startups |
| Retail AI Credit Cost | $10,000 | Typical bulk purchase or annual commitment |
| **Credex Discount (30%)** | **$3,000** | Total customer savings on the deal |
| Credex Margin | 15% of deal value | Commission/arbitrage on credit brokering |
| **Revenue per Customer** | **$1,500** | LTV of a single SpendLens conversion |

For every 10 customers SpendLens generates, Credex realizes **$15,000** in gross revenue.

## 3. CONVERSION FUNNEL MATH

The SpendLens funnel is optimized to filter for "High-Waste" users who are most likely to convert into Credex consultation clients.

*   **1,000 Visitors** (Top of Funnel)
*   **400 Complete Audit** (40% completion rate)
    *   *User friction is low; tool is fast and free.*
*   **150 Capture Email** (37.5% of auditors)
    *   *Requirement for the full PDF/Shareable report.*
*   **30 Book Consultation** (20% of leads)
    *   *Filtered for audits finding >$500/mo in potential savings.*
*   **10 Become Customers** (33% close rate)
    *   *Credex sales team closes high-intent savings leads.*

**Result:** From 1,000 visitors, we generate **$15,000 in revenue**.
**V-Value:** Each visitor is worth **$15.00**.

## 4. THE PATH TO $1M ARR (18-MONTH PLAN)

The scalability of SpendLens comes from the "Viral Audit Loop" where EMs share their savings results with other peers and founders.

| Period | Target Customers | Revenue (at $1,500/ea) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Month 1 - 3 | 100 | $150,000 |
| Month 4 - 6 | 200 | $300,000 |
| Month 7 - 12 | 400 | $600,000 |
| **TOTAL** | **700+** | **~$1,000,000+** |

### Critical Assumptions for Success:
1.  **Scale:** SpendLens must drive **10,000 audits per month** by Month 6.
2.  **Conversion:** We must maintain a **2% conversion rate** from total audits to Credex customers.
3.  **Deal Size:** The average credit deal size must stay at or above **$10,000**.
4.  **Virality:** The referral loop must drive at least **30% of all new audits** organically.

The operating costs of SpendLens are negligible compared to the revenue potential, thanks to the modern serverless stack.

*   **Monthly Operating Costs:** ~$50/month
    *   *Vercel Pro: $20*
    *   *Supabase (Base Tier): $25*
    *   *Resend (Free/Starter): $5*
*   **Revenue per Customer:** $1,500
*   **Break-even Point:** **1 customer per month.**

This tool pays for its entire annual operating budget with a single conversion. Every customer after the first one per month is 99%+ gross margin for the marketing budget.
